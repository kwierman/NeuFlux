Detailed Description

Chemical and isotopic definitions of the composition of rocks in South Dakota.

The chemical composition is as follows: 

  87.05   SiO3
  6.560   Al203
  0.840   Fe203
  1.243   MgO
  0.950   CaO
  3.008   Na2O
